Spyros Markopoulos was born in 1978 and grew up in Athens, Greece. He studied acting, singing and dancing in the “Drama School Theater of Art Karolos Koun” in Athens with teachers such as G. Lazanis, R. Pittaki, K. Gerou, S.Patroklos He has worked with various directors and choreographers in theater and in the opera in Athens and in Berlin such as M. Kougioumtzis, B. Nikolaidis, D. Piatas, S. Euaggelatos, Marco Arturo Marelli, Christof Loy, David Hermann,Opay L. Goldberg, Ramses Sigl, Martin Buczo, Maxine Braham in plays of Beckett, Goldoni, Lope de Vega, Euripides, Verdi, Xenakis, Veniadis, and on stages like Commedia, Amphitheater, Epidaurus, Deutsche Oper Berlin, Theater Verlängetes Wohnzimmer, Ackerstadtpalast. Since 2005 he lives and works in Berlin.
